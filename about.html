About Shea Tea Tree

Rooted in nature. Made for real routines.

Shea Tea Tree brings botanical inspiration into simple, accessible daily hair care.

Company introduction

Shea Tea Tree is a natural hair care brand focused on shampoo and conditioner products inspired by familiar botanical extracts and designed for straightforward daily routines.

Our story

The brand began with a simple idea: customers should not need a complicated routine to care for their hair. Shea Tea Tree was created to bring together recognizable ingredients, clear product benefits and easy guidance for cleansing, conditioning and maintaining moisture.

Mission

Our mission

To make dependable, easy-to-understand hair care available to customers who want simple routines, botanical inspiration and consistent product quality.

Vision

Our vision

To become a trusted international hair care brand known for accessible products, honest communication and practical education.

Brand values

What guides us

ClarityStraightforward product information and easy routines.
QualityCareful formulation, responsible manufacturing and consistent standards.
CareA customer-first approach across products, service and education.

Product philosophy

Our product philosophy centers on simple routines, familiar botanical extracts and benefits customers can understand. Every formula is designed to play a clear role in a complete wash-day routine.

Commitment to quality

We work with manufacturing partners that follow documented production, testing and quality-control processes. Products are reviewed for consistency, packaging integrity and customer usability before release.

Made in USA

Made in USA

Shea Tea Tree products are manufactured and packaged in the United States using quality-controlled processes and globally sourced ingredients.

Company FAQ

About Shea Tea Tree

What is Shea Tea Tree?

Shea Tea Tree is a natural hair care brand offering shampoo and conditioner products inspired by coconut, rosemary, tea tree and shea butter.

What types of products does the company offer?

The current collection includes sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners for cleansing, hydration, softness and scalp comfort.

What are the company’s values?

Clarity, quality and customer care guide the brand’s product development, communication and service.

Where are the products made?

The products are manufactured and packaged in the United States.
