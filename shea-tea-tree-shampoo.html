A practical guide to choosing the right Shea Tea Tree Shampoo for dry, oily, curly, textured, dull, or frequently washed hair.

What Is Shea Tea Tree Shampoo?

Shea Tea Tree Shampoo is a botanical hair-care product created to cleanse the scalp and hair without leaving them feeling excessively dry. The collection includes formulas made with tea tree extract, rosemary extract, coconut extract, jojoba oil, and Moroccan argan oil.

Each formula is designed for a different hair-care need, but all focus on gentle cleansing, moisture support, softness, manageability, and healthy-looking shine. The products are presented as sulfate-free, 100% vegan, and available in a large 1000 ml pump bottle.

Is Shea Tea Tree Shampoo Sulfate-Free?

Direct answerYes. Shea Tea Tree Shampoo is presented as sulfate-free.

A sulfate-free shampoo may suit people whose hair feels dry, coarse, curly, textured, colored, or chemically treated, as well as anyone who prefers a gentler cleansing routine.

How Do You Use Shea Tea Tree Shampoo?

  1. Wet the hair and scalp thoroughly.
  2. Apply an appropriate amount of shampoo.
  3. Massage gently into the scalp using your fingertips.
  4. Work the lather through the hair.
  5. Rinse thoroughly.
  6. Repeat when necessary.
  7. Follow with a matching Shea Tea Tree Conditioner.

How Often Should You Use It?

The ideal washing frequency depends on your hair type, scalp condition, lifestyle, and product use.

  • Oily scalp: every 1–2 days or as needed
  • Normal hair: 2–3 times per week
  • Dry or textured hair: 1–2 times per week
  • Heavy product buildup: whenever more thorough cleansing is needed
