Product description

Shea Tea Tree Coconut Extract Shampoo is designed for hair that feels dry, dull or difficult to manage. Its gentle cleansing system removes everyday buildup while helping hair feel softer and look more polished after washing.

Main features

  • Sulfate-free cleansing system
  • Helps support hydration and shine
  • Suitable for regular wash routines
  • Botanical-inspired coconut extract
  • Fresh, clean fragrance

Ingredients

Water, Sodium Lauroyl Methyl Isethionate, Cocamidopropyl Betaine, Glycerin, Cocos Nucifera (Coconut) Fruit Extract, Panthenol, Hydrolyzed Wheat Protein, Polyquaternium-10, Citric Acid, Sodium Benzoate, Potassium Sorbate, Fragrance.

Directions for use

  1. Wet hair and scalp thoroughly.
  2. Apply a small amount and massage gently into the scalp.
  3. Work the lather through the lengths without rough scrubbing.
  4. Rinse thoroughly and repeat when needed.
  5. Follow with Shea Tea Tree conditioner.

Frequently asked questions

Coconut Shampoo FAQ

Is this shampoo sulfate-free?

Yes. The demo formula uses a sulfate-free cleansing system.

What hair type is it suitable for?

It is designed for normal, dry and textured hair that needs gentle cleansing and moisture support.

How often should I use it?

Most customers can use it one to three times per week, depending on scalp oil, product buildup and styling habits.

Can it be used on color-treated hair?

The sulfate-free system is intended to be gentle, but customers should always follow their colorist’s care instructions.
